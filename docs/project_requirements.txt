DEVELOPMENT OF A MEMBERSHIP REFERRAL PORTAL / SYSTEM
ATCLSACCOS/NC/02/2025-2026
1. BACKGROUND
ATCL SACCOS LTD intends to enhance its membership growth strategy and service delivery through the
development of a Membership Referral Portal/System.
The system is expected to enable existing members to refer new members digitally, track referrals, and
generate management reports.
Qualified and experienced service providers are hereby invited to submit Quotations for the design,
development, testing, deployment, and support of the Membership Referral Portal/System.
2. OBJECTIVE OF THE PROCUREMENT
The objective of this procurement is to:
• Develop a secure, user-friendly membership referral portal/system
• Improve membership acquisition, tracking, and reporting
• Ensure value for money through competitive quotation analysis
• Select a competent service provider in line with procurement procedures of ATCL SACCOS LTD
3. METHOD OF PROCUREMENT
The method of procurement for this assignment shall be Request for Quotations (RFQ), and the
selection shall be conducted through Quotation Analysis, in accordance with applicable procurement
policies and procedures.
4. SCOPE OF SERVICES
The successful bidder shall be responsible for:
• System analysis and requirements gathering
• Design and development of the Membership Referral Portal/System
• Integration with existing systems (where applicable)
• User acceptance testing and deployment
• User training and basic documentation
• Warranty and post-implementation technical support
5. MINIMUM SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS AND FEATURES
Bidders shall quote have based on the following minimum requirements or better:
(a) Functional Requirements
• Member registration and login
• Referral submission and tracking
• Unique referral identification
• Approval workflow for referred members
• Referral performance dashboard
• Automated notifications (SMS/Email where applicable)
• Administrative control panel
• Report generation (daily, monthly, annual)
(b) Technical Requirements
• Web-based system (desktop and mobile compatible)
• Secure authentication and data protection
• Scalable architecture
• Backup and recovery mechanism
• Compliance with ICT and data protection best practices
6. DELIVERABLES
The expected deliverables include:
1. Fully functional Membership Referral Portal/System
2. Source code and system documentation
3. User manuals and administrator guides
4. Training for designated staff
5. Warranty and support agreement
7. ELIGIBILITY AND QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS
Bidders must submit the following:
1. Valid Business Registration Certificate
2. Tax Identification Number (TIN)
3. Valid Tax Clearance Certificate
4. Company profile detailing ICT/system development experience
5. Evidence of similar systems developed (references or portfolio)
8. QUOTATION SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS
Each quotation must clearly include:
• Total cost (inclusive of all applicable taxes)
• Implementation timeline
• Warranty and support period
• Payment terms
• Quotation validity period (minimum 30 days)
9. EVALUATION CRITERIA (QUOTATION ANALYSIS)
Quotations shall be evaluated based on the following criteria:
Criteria Consideration
Compliance with scope and specifications Mandatory
Price competitiveness High
Technical capability and system quality High
Implementation timeline Medium
Relevant experience and references Medium
The contract shall be awarded to the lowest evaluated, responsive, and compliant bidder, and not
necessarily the lowest price.
10. IMPLEMENTATION PERIOD
The successful bidder shall complete development and deployment of the system within [45] days from
the date of contract signing or issuance of Purchase Order.
